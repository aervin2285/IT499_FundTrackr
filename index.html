About Us

FundTrackr was created to make fundraising easier for schools, sports teams, and local organizations. We believe fundraising should be simple, organized, and stress-free — and that's what we deliver. Our platform gives teams the tools to launch, manage, and track their fundraising efforts all in one place.

How It Works

  1. Pick a Package – Choose from our Starter, Standard, or Premium fundraising packages.
  2. Customize Your Fundraiser – Add your items, goals, and team info.
  3. Track Progress – Use our dashboard to see how you're doing in real-time.
  4. Share and Succeed – Promote your fundraiser and reach your goals!
